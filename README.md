## 📖 About the Project

**Tata Car Lift** is a professional car lift service providing comfortable and affordable transportation between **Dubai** and **Abu Dhabi**. Whether you're a daily commuter, a student, or a tourist exploring the UAE, we offer reliable shared and private rides tailored to your needs.

Our service bridges the gap between expensive taxis and time-consuming public transport, offering the perfect balance of convenience, safety, and affordability.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **🚘 Shared & Private Rides** – Choose between shared carpooling or exclusive private vehicles.
- **💰 Affordable Pricing** – Transparent rates with no hidden charges:
  - Shared ride: AED 90–100 per person
  - Private ride: AED 190–210
  - SUV options available for groups (AED 240–360)
- **🛡️ Professional Drivers** – Licensed and experienced drivers with excellent knowledge of UAE roads.
- **📍 Flexible Pickup/Drop-off** – Convenient locations across both Dubai and Abu Dhabi.
- **⏰ Punctual Service** – Reliable scheduling, perfect for daily commuters.
- **🧳 Spacious Vehicles** – Options for solo travelers, families, and groups up to 12 passengers.
